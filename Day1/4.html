Gourmet Delight: Truffle Risotto

Description

Experience the rich and creamy texture of our Truffle Risotto, a luxurious dish made with Arborio rice, fresh truffles, and a blend of savory herbs.

Ingredients

  • Arborio rice
  • Fresh truffles
  • Vegetable broth
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Butter
  • Shallots
  • White wine
  • Salt and pepper

Preparation Method

Our chefs start by sautéing shallots in butter, then add Arborio rice to toast it lightly. Gradually, we incorporate vegetable broth and white wine while stirring continuously until the rice is al dente. Finally, we fold in freshly grated Parmesan cheese and shaved truffles for that exquisite finish.

Pairing Suggestions

This dish pairs beautifully with a crisp Sauvignon Blanc or a light Pinot Noir, enhancing the earthy flavors of the truffles.

Chef's Recommendation

For an elevated experience, consider adding a poached egg on top, allowing the yolk to enrich the risotto further.
